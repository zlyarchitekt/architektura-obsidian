## Summary
In this essay from Kwartalnik RZUT, architect Mikołaj Smoleński critiques the over-regulated, sanitized, and unimaginative state of contemporary playgrounds. He contrasts the freedom and inherent risk of unstructured play spaces from the 1980s with today's bureaucratically stifled "Guantanamo gardens." Smoleński proposes several design strategies to circumvent these limitations, such as designing "non-playgrounds," leveraging architectural surfaces (like asphalt), and creating objects that invite play without being legally classified as play equipment.

**PL:** *W eseju z Kwartalnika RZUT architekt Mikołaj Smoleński krytykuje przeregulowany, sterylny i pozbawiony wyobraźni stan współczesnych placów zabaw. Zestawia swobodę i wpisane w nią ryzyko nieustrukturyzowanych przestrzeni z lat 80. z dzisiejszymi, zduszonymi biurokracją "ogródkami Guantanamo". Smoleński proponuje strategie projektowe omijające te ograniczenia, takie jak "nie-place zabaw", wykorzystanie nawierzchni architektonicznych i obiektów nienazywanych formalnie urządzeniami zabawowymi.*

## Key Points
- Generational shift: The generation that enjoyed unstructured, risky play in the 1980s is now manufacturing highly regulated, fenced, and unimaginative spaces for their children.
- The "4S Formula" (swing, see-saw, slide, sandbox) limits the definition of play spaces to a standard, uninspiring set.
- Risk is a crucial component of play that is actively designed out of contemporary spaces due to liability fears.
- "Guantanamo Gardens" (Ogródki Guantanamo): The phenomenon of micro-playgrounds enclosed by panel fences solely to meet legal residential building requirements.
- Design strategies to bypass constraints:
  - Allowing play on standard architectural elements (stairs, ramps).
  - Creating art installations or "non-playgrounds" (nie-place zabaw) that aren't legally classified as playground equipment, avoiding restrictive safety certifications.
  - Designing with architectural surfaces (smooth concrete, asphalt) that naturally invite spontaneous play (chalk, rollerblading) without liability issues.
