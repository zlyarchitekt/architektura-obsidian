## Definition
An acronym defining the standard, unimaginative baseline of modern playground design: Swing, See-saw, Slide, Sandbox.

**PL:** *Akronim definiujący standardową, pozbawioną wyobraźni bazę nowoczesnego projektowania placów zabaw: Swing (huśtawka), See-saw (ważka), Slide (zjeżdżalnia), Sandbox (piaskownica).*

## Historical Context
As play spaces became industrialized and heavily regulated by safety certifications, spontaneous structures were replaced by catalog equipment. The 4S formula represents the minimum viable product for municipal and developer play space planning.
