## Definition
The use of machine-learning models — notably text-to-image generators such as Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, and Midjourney — to produce architectural visualisations, spatial proposals, or critical imagery. Beyond pure representation, some practitioners deploy these tools as speculative design instruments capable of estranging familiar typologies and revealing hidden biases embedded in training datasets.

**PL:** *Wykorzystanie modeli uczenia maszynowego — w szczególności generatorów text-to-image takich jak Stable Diffusion, DALL-E i Midjourney — do produkcji wizualizacji architektonicznych, propozycji przestrzennych lub obrazów krytycznych. Poza czystą reprezentacją niektórzy praktycy wdrażają te narzędzia jako instrumenty projektowania spekulatywnego zdolne do wyobcowania znanych typologii i ujawnienia ukrytych uprzedzeń zawartych w zbiorach treningowych.*

## Historical Context
Generative adversarial networks (GANs) appeared in architecture schools around 2015; the public launch of Stable Diffusion and Midjourney in 2022 democratised access, triggering both enthusiastic adoption and sharp critique. Architects worry about copyright, labour displacement, and the reinforcement of conservative formal stereotypes through dataset bias. Conversely, artists and critics have embraced the "uncanny" quality of AI output as a productive estrangement device.

## Key Proponents
- [[Noviki]] — Polish collective using Stable Diffusion to critique gendered public toilets.
- Academic researchers in computational design (e.g. Stanislas Chaillou, Daniel Bolojan) who explore latent-space navigation for plan generation.

## Key Examples
- **Stabilna dyfuzja toalet publicznych** (Noviki, 2023) — uses AI-generated toilet interiors as a critical medium rather than a design proposal.

## Critiques & Alternatives
- **Dataset bias**: AI models trained on internet images reproduce dominant architectural styles and marginalise vernacular, non-Western, or feminist spatial practices.
- **Authorship and labour**: Generative tools obscure the labour of photographers, draughtspersons, and modellers whose work constitutes the training data.
- **Estrangement potential**: The dream-like, physically impossible qualities of AI imagery can be harnessed to defamiliarise normative typologies — as in Noviki's work — turning representation into critique.
