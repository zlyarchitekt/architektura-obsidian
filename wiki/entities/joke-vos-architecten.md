## Overview

Joke Vos Architecten is a Rotterdam-based firm known for expressive waterfront housing that reinterprets the garden as a water-based experience. Their Periscope Houses (2002) on Rotterdam's Nesselande peninsula feature four masses resembling periscopes, with jetties, floating docks, and dining terraces on the water. The flexible ground plan allows the kitchen to be placed on either floor, and rooms can be divided as needs change — a rare example of genuine adaptability in low-rise housing.
