## Overview

Prof. Elżbieta Trocka-Leszczyńska is a Polish architect and academic at the Wydział Architektury Politechniki Wrocławskiej (WA PWr). She holds the distinction of being the first woman to serve in the leadership of WA PWr — initially as vice-dean (from 1993), then as dean for multiple terms. This appointment came 45 years after the faculty was founded in 1948, during which period not a single woman served in any faculty leadership role.
