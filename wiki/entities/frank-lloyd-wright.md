## Overview
Renowned independent American architect known for organic architecture, Prairie Houses, and Fallingwater.

**PL:** *Znakomity amerykański architekt znany z architektury organicznej, "Domów Preriowych" i Fallingwater.*


## Key Works / Projects
- **Suntop Homes** (Ardmore, Pennsylvania, 1938–42) — Quadruple housing type with 90-degree rotation; pinwheel privacy system; only one unit built due to WWII.
- [[Robie House]] (1909)
- [[Fallingwater]] (1937)
- [[guggenheim-museum|Guggenheim Museum]] (1959)
