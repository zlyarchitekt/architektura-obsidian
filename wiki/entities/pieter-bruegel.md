## Overview
Flemish Renaissance painter and printmaker, known for his landscapes and peasant scenes.

**PL:** *Flamandzki malarz i grafik epoki renesansu, znany z pejzaży i scen z życia chłopów.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Children's Games** (*Zabawy dziecięce*, 1560) — painting depicting over 200 children playing various games in a townscape.

## Ideas & Influences
Culturally represents an archetypal vision of unstructured, collective, and somewhat chaotic public play utilizing the entire urban environment.

## In the Sources
- His painting *Children's Games* is cited as capturing the atmosphere of total freedom and unstructured play that characterized 1980s Polish housing estates in [[rzut-gra-w-dwa-ognie]].
