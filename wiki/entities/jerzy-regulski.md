## Overview
Polish economist, academic, and key architect of the 1990 restoration of local self-government in Poland.

**PL:** *Polski ekonomista, naukowiec i główny architekt przywrócenia w Polsce samorządu terytorialnego w 1990 roku.*

## Key Works / Projects
- Co-author of the 1990 Act on Municipal Self-Government (Ustawa o samorządzie gminnym).

## Ideas & Influences
Instrumental in establishing "spatial order" (*ład przestrzenny*) as the first and primary foundational task of restored municipal governments. He believed there was no contradiction between spatial order and socio-economic investment, as the two must be integrated.

## In the Sources
- Cited by Grzegorz A. Buczek as the promoter who introduced the concept of *ład przestrzenny* into the self-government act in [[rzut-nielad-w-prawie]].
