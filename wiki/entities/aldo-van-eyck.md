## Overview
Dutch architect, a leading protagonist of the Structuralist movement and a key member of Team 10. He designed over 700 playgrounds in Amsterdam after World War II, using simple, abstract geometric forms.

**PL:** *Holenderski architekt, czołowy przedstawiciel strukturalizmu i kluczowy członek Team 10. Zaprojektował ponad 700 placów zabaw w Amsterdamie po II wojnie światowej.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Amsterdam Playgrounds** (1947–1978) — a network of over 700 public playgrounds utilizing minimal, abstract geometric elements.
- **Municipal Orphanage** (Amsterdam, 1960) — defining work of structuralist architecture.

## Ideas & Influences
Believed that architecture should facilitate social interaction. His playgrounds provided abstract frameworks (like his famous concrete sandpits and metal climbing frames) that encouraged children's imaginations rather than prescribing a specific type of play.
