## Overview
Polish landscape architect and author who writes about urban ecology, biodiversity, and the shifting role of design in the context of the Anthropocene.

**PL:** *Polska architektka krajobrazu i autorka pisząca o ekologii miejskiej, bioróżnorodności i zmieniającej się roli projektowania w kontekście Antropocenu.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Ścieżki londyńskich lisów** (2026) — essay in Kwartalnik RZUT on urban foxes and the "third landscape" [[rzut-sciezki-londynskich-lisow]]

## Ideas & Influences
Advocates for the idea of the landscape architect as a "landscape physician" who follows the principle of *primum non nocere* (first, do no harm) when intervening in urban ecosystems.

## In the Sources
- Argues for seeing the city as an ecosystem with non-human stakeholders in [[rzut-sciezki-londynskich-lisow]].
