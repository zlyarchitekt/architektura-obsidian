## Overview
Polish architect and co-author (with Marta Wróblewska) of "Bezpieczeństwo i higiena pracy. O przestrzeniach seksworkingowych" (RZUT-34, 2023). The essay examines sex-work spaces from an architectural and pro-sex feminist perspective, surveying typologies across Europe and arguing for spatial design that treats sex work as legitimate labour requiring occupational safety.

## Ideas & Influences
Drawing on Juno Mac and Molly Smith's *Rewolta prostytutek*, Mazur and Wróblewska frame sex work as labour rather than moral failing. Their architectural analysis reveals how each spatial typology (drive-in, window, club, Laufhaus, escort flat) produces distinct safety affordances and risks, and how criminalisation removes workers from the planning processes that shape their workplaces.
