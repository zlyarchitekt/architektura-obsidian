## Overview

Karol Szparkowski is a Polish architect and tutor for the Mood for Wood workshops. Described by founder Magda Wypusz as her favourite tutor who "always pushes us forward," he has led multiple teams producing highly popular public structures. In 2019 his group built a bird observatory over Lake Rusałka (Poznań), which became one of the most visited spots on the lake. In 2020 his team created three large multi-functional platforms for the Strzelnica Cultural Centre in Cieszyn, still in daily use by residents.
