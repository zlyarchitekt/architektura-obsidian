## Overview

Polish architect, born 1959. Graduate of the Faculty of Architecture, Warsaw University of Technology (WAPW). Co-founder and partner of [[JEMS Architekci]] (1988–present). Previously worked in the ESPEA cooperative (1984–1988). Teaches design at WAPW since 2008 and has been a thesis promoter at the Faculty of Architecture, Poznań University of Technology since 2004.

**PL:** *Polski architekt, ur. 1959. Absolwent WAPW. Współzałożyciel i wspólnik [[JEMS Architekci]] (od 1988). Uprzednio w spółdzielni ESPEA (1984–1988). Od 2008 prowadzi zajęcia na WAPW; od 2004 promotor prac dyplomowych na Politechnice Poznańskiej.*
