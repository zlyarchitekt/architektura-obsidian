## Overview
Lu Wenyu is a Chinese architect and co-founder (with her husband [[Wang Shu]]) of Amateur Architecture Studio. She played an equal role in the firm's critical regionalist designs, such as the Ningbo Museum, but was excluded from the 2012 Pritzker Architecture Prize, which was awarded solely to Wang Shu.

**PL:** Lu Wenyu to chińska architektka i współzałożycielka (wraz z mężem Wang Shu) Amateur Architecture Studio. Odegrała równą rolę w krytycznoregjionalistycznych projektach firmy, takich jak Muzeum w Ningbo, ale została wykluczona z Nagrody Pritzkera w 2012 r., którą przyznano wyłącznie Wang Shu.

## Key Works / Projects
- **Ningbo Museum** (2008, Ningbo)
- **Xiangshan Campus, China Academy of Art** (Hangzhou)

## Ideas & Influences
Alongside Wang Shu, her work focuses on rescuing the material memory of Chinese villages from rapid demolition by reusing salvaged bricks and tiles in modern architectural forms.
