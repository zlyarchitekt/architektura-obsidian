## Overview

The Periscope Houses sit at the entrance of Rotterdam's Nesselande residential belt on an artificial peninsula surrounded by water on three sides. Four masses reminiscent of periscopes meld three apartments into one, clinging to the shore. Like buds on a branch, three-storey flats seem to grow out of dry land and can be approached by land or over water. The garden is re-interpreted as water: a jetty floating on the water is divided into two levels, with a dining terrace above and a fishing dock below. Bobbing boxes on the lower level end in a loggia, like a periscope turning toward the view. The flexibly adjustable ground plan serves changing owners' needs; only the staircase and water facilities are fixed. The kitchen may be installed on ground or first floor.

Elastyczny plan parteru służy zmieniającym się potrzebom właścicieli.
