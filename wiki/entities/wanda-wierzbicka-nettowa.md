## Overview

Wanda Wierzbicka-Nettowa (1892–1975) was one of the two first women to graduate from a Polish architecture school — she received her diploma from the Wydział Architektury Politechniki Warszawskiej in 1922, alongside [[Jadwiga Dobrzyńska]]. Unlike Dobrzyńska, who became a recognised professional icon, Wierzbicka-Nettowa is today largely forgotten. Her obscurity is itself historically significant: it illustrates how the same structural barriers that made any woman's architecture career exceptional in interwar Poland also determined whose exception was preserved by history and whose was not.

**PL:** Wanda Wierzbicka-Nettowa (1892–1975) — jedna z dwóch pierwszych kobiet z dyplomem architektonicznym w Polsce (WA PW, 1922), wraz z Jadwigą Dobrzyńską. W przeciwieństwie do Dobrzyńskiej, dziś niemal zapomniana — co samo w sobie jest historycznie znaczące.
