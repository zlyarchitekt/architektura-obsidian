## Overview

Aleksandra Gryc is a Polish architect, researcher, and exhibition designer. She is a graduate of the Cracow University of Technology (Politechnika Krakowska), with additional studies in Vienna and Berlin, and a postgraduate degree in service design from SWPS Warsaw. She worked in the Tokyo office of Sou Fujimoto and in Berlin and Polish architectural practices. For eight years she has served as the guardian (opiekunka) of OSSA (Ogólnopolskie Spotkania Studentów Architektury) workshops. She currently lives in Warsaw and designs exhibitions. She is a firm believer in collectivity and in women. Her statistical analysis "Architektki na Uczelniach" (RZUT-34, 2023) introduced the Glass Ceiling Index (GCI, Eurostat methodology) and the flow index (*indeks przepływów*) to Polish architectural discourse, documenting both the extreme feminisation of architecture education and the persistent structural inequality at professorial level.
