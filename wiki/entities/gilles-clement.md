## Overview
French gardener, landscape architect, botanist, and essayist. He is best known for his theories of the "Planetary Garden" (Jardin Planétaire) and the "Third Landscape" (Tiers Paysage).

**PL:** *Francuski ogrodnik, architekt krajobrazu, botanik i eseista. Najbardziej znany jako twórca teorii "Ogrodu Planetarnego" oraz "Trzeciego Krajobrazu".*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Parc André-Citroën** (1992) — Paris park co-designed with Alain Provost, Patrick Berger, Jean-Paul Viguier, and François Jodry.
- **Manifeste du Tiers paysage** (2004) — manifesto outlining the ecological value of abandoned or marginal lands.

## Ideas & Influences
His concept of the *Third Landscape* emphasizes the ecological richness of "undecided" spaces—abandoned lands, roadsides, and urban margins—which act as refuges for biodiversity away from human interference.
