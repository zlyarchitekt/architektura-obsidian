## Overview
Polish architect / researcher and photographer, associated with the preservation of post-war modernist architecture in the "Modernizm w Lublinie" group.

**PL:** *Polski architekt / badacz i fotograf, związany z ochroną powojennej architektury modernistycznej w grupie „Modernizm w Lublinie”.*

## Key Works / Projects
- Co-author of an essay analyzing the conflict between modernism and contemporary building codes.

## Ideas & Influences
Highlighting the destructive impact of absolute compliance with modern fire and thermal codes on heritage structures that are not formally protected.
