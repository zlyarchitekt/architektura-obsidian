## Overview

MNLA (Mathews Nielsen Landscape Architects) is a New York-based landscape architecture firm. The practice focuses on urban public space, waterfront design, ecological restoration, and community infrastructure. Founded by Mary Miss Nielsen and others in 1999, MNLA has built a reputation for landscape projects at the intersection of ecology and urbanism, particularly in New York City's waterfront context.

## Key Works / Projects

- **Little Island** (2021, New York) — landscape architecture co-designer with [[Heatherwick Studio]] and [[Arup]]; responsible for the planting programme (100+ indigenous species) and the experiential design of paths, terraces, and gathering spaces. [[Little Island]]
