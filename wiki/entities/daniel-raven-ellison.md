## Overview
British "guerrilla geographer" and National Geographic Explorer. He is the founder of the campaign to make London the world's first National Park City.

**PL:** *Brytyjski "geograf partyzancki", badacz National Geographic. Założyciel kampanii mającej na celu uczynienie z Londynu pierwszego na świecie Miasta-Parku Narodowego.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **London National Park City** (2019) — successful campaign to designate London as a National Park City.

## Ideas & Influences
Promotes the idea that cities should be treated, protected, and celebrated with the same reverence as national parks, emphasizing biodiversity and human connection to urban nature.

## In the Sources
- Mentioned as the driver behind the London National Park City foundation, which trains Urban Park Rangers to build respect for urban nature in [[rzut-sciezki-londynskich-lisow]].
