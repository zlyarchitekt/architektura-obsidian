## Overview
A Kraków-based design studio specializing in spaces for children, specifically advocating for alternative, non-standard approaches to playground design.

**PL:** *Krakowska pracownia projektowa specjalizująca się w przestrzeniach dla dzieci, promująca alternatywne, niestandardowe podejście do projektowania placów zabaw.*

## Key Works / Projects

## Ideas & Influences
Coined or heavily promoted the term **"non-playground" (nie-plac zabaw)** as a strategy to create sculptural, interactive public elements that invite play without falling under the restrictive legal definitions and health-and-safety regulations of formal playgrounds.

## In the Sources
- Mentioned as pioneers of the "non-playground" strategy in Poland in [[rzut-gra-w-dwa-ognie]].
