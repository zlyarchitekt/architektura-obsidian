## Overview

Maurice Nio is a Dutch architect known for bold, futuristic housing designs that engage infrastructure and sensory experience. His Cyclops project in Rotterdam (2001) conceals twelve residential units behind a translucent, sound-absorbing street wall, resolving the tension between urban density and traffic noise through sectional ingenuity. The saw-tooth upper storey and brick-clad retaining-wall base create a striking sectional profile, while private courtyards connect to a communal green.
