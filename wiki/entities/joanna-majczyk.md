## Overview

Joanna Majczyk is a Polish architectural historian associated with Politechnika Wrocławska. She co-authored "100 Lat Architektek: Architektura i 'Kwestia Kobieca'" (RZUT-34, 2023) with [[Agnieszka Tomaszewicz]] — a historical survey of 100+ years of women's presence in Polish architecture. Her research focuses on the history of women in the Polish architectural profession, including statistical analysis and archival recovery of forgotten figures. Co-author with Tomaszewicz of "Pioneers. Polish modernist female architects. The centre and peripheries" (Docomomo International, Valencia, 2022).

**PL:** Polska historyczka architektury, Politechnika Wrocławska. Współautorka (z Agnieszką Tomaszewicz) artykułu "100 Lat Architektek" (RZUT-34, 2023) i "Pioneers. Polish modernist female architects" (Docomomo, 2022). Badaczka historii kobiet w polskim zawodzie architektonicznym.
