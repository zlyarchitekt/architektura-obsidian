## Overview

Polish architect, born 1965. Graduate of WAPW. Joined [[JEMS Architekci]] in 1993, became partner in 1996 and co-owner. Has taught design at the Warsaw Faculty of Architecture (WAPW) since 2008. One of the four partners individually awarded the Honorowa Nagroda SARP in 2012 for contribution to the development of Polish architecture.

**PL:** *Polski architekt, ur. 1965. Absolwent WAPW. W JEMS od 1993, wspólnik od 1996. Od 2008 prowadzi zajęcia na WAPW. Jeden z czterech partnerów indywidualnie nagrodzonych Honorową Nagrodą SARP 2012.*
