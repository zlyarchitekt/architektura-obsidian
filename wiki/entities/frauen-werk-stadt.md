## Overview
A pioneering social housing project in Vienna designed exclusively by and for women. Completed in 1997, it serves as an iconic, large-scale implementation of gender mainstreaming in urban planning.

**PL:** *Pionierski projekt budownictwa socjalnego w Wiedniu, zaprojektowany wyłącznie przez kobiety z myślą o kobietach. Ukończony w 1997 roku, stanowi ikoniczne, wielkoskalowe wdrożenie nurtu gender mainstreaming w planowaniu przestrzennym.*

## Ideas & Influences
The urban design actively answers the daily needs of caregivers (statistically mostly women) through proximity to public transport, wide pram-friendly pathways, well-lit social spaces with high visibility for children's safety, and integrated kindergartens and community areas.

## In the Sources
- Mentioned as the foremost Austrian example of gender mainstreaming that shares a conceptual kinship with Iwona Buczkowska's socially oriented timber estate in France in [[rzut-bal-architektoniczki]].
