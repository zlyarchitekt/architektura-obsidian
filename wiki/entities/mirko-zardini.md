## Overview
Italian architect, author, and curator. Served as the director of the Canadian Centre for Architecture (CCA) in Montreal.

**PL:** *Włoski architekt, autor i kurator. Pełnił funkcję dyrektora Canadian Centre for Architecture (CCA) w Montrealu.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Sense of the City** (2005) — exhibition and publication at the CCA exploring the sensory dimensions of urban environments.

## Ideas & Influences
In his essay "Surface of the City," Zardini argues for reconsidering materials like asphalt not just as infrastructure for cars, but as vital, collective surfaces for pedestrians, cyclists, and urban life.

## In the Sources
- His defense of asphalt as a communal, pedestrian surface is cited to support the use of "architectural surfaces" for children's spontaneous play in [[rzut-gra-w-dwa-ognie]].
