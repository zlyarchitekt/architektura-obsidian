## Overview
Polish architect, associated with the Wrocław-based CH+ Architekci studio, and commentator on urban design and public spaces. 

**PL:** *Polski architekt związany z wrocławską pracownią CH+ Architekci, komentator przestrzeni publicznych i urbanistyki.*

## Key Works / Projects
- **Housing at Nowe Żerniki** (Wrocław) — residential project featuring creative use of architectural surfaces in lieu of traditional playgrounds.

## Ideas & Influences
Advocates for moving away from over-regulated, sanitized playgrounds toward more integrated, risk-tolerant, and imaginative public spaces that do not rely on standard catalog equipment.

## In the Sources
- Author of "Gra w dwa ognie," critiquing contemporary playgrounds and offering workarounds like "non-playgrounds" in [[rzut-gra-w-dwa-ognie]].
