## Overview

NIO Architects is a Rotterdam-based firm known for experimental, compact housing that challenges conventional domestic scale and form. Their Groenoord-Zuid project in Schiedam (2001) takes the shape of a UFO-like stacked mass, decreasing in elements from ground to roof, with eight small patios at ground level and aviary-like upper boxes. The project turns inward with extreme compactness despite the openness of its park setting, producing a structure that reads as a game of building blocks.
