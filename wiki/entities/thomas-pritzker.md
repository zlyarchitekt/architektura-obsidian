## Overview
Thomas Pritzker is an American businessman, heir to the Pritzker family fortune, and executive chairman of Hyatt Hotels. He has been deeply involved in the administration of the [[Pritzker Architecture Prize]], founded by his family. In 2026, he faced intense scrutiny and resigned from his executive role at Hyatt following revelations in legal documents connecting him to Jeffrey Epstein, causing a crisis of moral authority for the architecture prize.
