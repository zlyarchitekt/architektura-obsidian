## Overview

Virgilio Gutierrez Herreros is a Spanish architect who co-designed the La Mareta housing estate in Tenerife with Eustaquio Martinez Garcia (2002). The project uses thick L-shaped walls and roofs as protective plates against unfavourable winds, while opening completely to southwestern views. The development re-evaluates the sandy, coastal environment through tectonic rather than mimetic means.

**PL:** Virgilio Gutierrez Herreros to hiszpański architekt, który współzaprojektował osiedle La Mareta na Teneryfie z Eustaquio Martinez Garcia (2002).
