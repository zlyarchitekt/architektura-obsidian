## Overview

Agnieszka Tomaszewicz is a Polish architectural historian associated with Politechnika Wrocławska. She co-authored "100 Lat Architektek: Architektura i 'Kwestia Kobieca'" (RZUT-34, 2023) with [[Joanna Majczyk]] — a historical survey of 100+ years of women's presence in Polish architecture. Co-author with Majczyk of "Pioneers. Polish modernist female architects. The centre and peripheries" (Docomomo International, Valencia, 2022).

**PL:** Polska historyczka architektury, Politechnika Wrocławska. Współautorka (z Joanną Majczyk) artykułu "100 Lat Architektek" (RZUT-34, 2023) i "Pioneers. Polish modernist female architects" (Docomomo, 2022).
