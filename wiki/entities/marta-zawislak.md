## Overview
Polish architect / researcher, associated with the "Modernizm w Lublinie" group, which advocates for the preservation of post-war modernist architecture in Lublin.

**PL:** *Polska architektka / badaczka, związana z grupą „Modernizm w Lublinie”, która aktywnie działa na rzecz zachowania powojennej architektury modernistycznej w Lublinie.*

## Key Works / Projects
- Co-author of an essay analyzing the conflict between modernism and contemporary building codes. 

## Ideas & Influences
Advocates for the thoughtful structural modernization (like varying insulation thickness) of unprotected modernist buildings to preserve their tectonic values.
