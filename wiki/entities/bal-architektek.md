## Overview
A Polish architectural collective and activist group founded by Dominika Janicka, Dominika Wilczyńska, and Barbara Nawrocka. They advocate for gender equality in the architectural profession and promote feminist approaches to spatial design.

**PL:** *Polski kolektyw architektoniczny i grupa aktywistyczna założona przez Dominikę Janicką, Dominikę Wilczyńską i Barbarę Nawrocką. Promują równość płci w zawodzie architekta oraz feministyczne podejście do projektowania przestrzeni.*

## Key Works / Projects
- Advocacy campaigns identifying the lack of feminatives (architektka) in Polish architectural offices.
- Educational panels and publications breaking down the patriarchal status quo in architectural education and practice.

## Ideas & Influences
Promotes intersectional feminism in spatial design. Challenges the "starchitect" model by highlighting collaborative teamwork and exposing the structural disadvantages women face in the field, such as emotional procrastination and the unequal burden of care work.
