## Overview
Polish architect, urbanist, academic lecturer, and former local government official (including serving as a burmistrz / mayor). A recognized expert on spatial planning law and municipal governance.

**PL:** *Polski architekt, urbanista, wykładowca akademicki i były urzędnik samorządowy (w tym burmistrz). Uznany ekspert w dziedzinie prawa planowania przestrzennego i zarządzania gminą.*

## Key Works / Projects
- Advisor to relevant ministries during the drafting of spatial planning legislation, active voice in public debate regarding spatial order in Poland.

## Ideas & Influences
Strong critic of the current interpretation of planning laws in Poland. He advocates for a hierarchical reading of law—insisting that the Act on Municipal Self-Government (which bounds the executive to council resolutions like the *Studium*) should override the "flat" interpretations of administrative courts that currently allow *Warunki Zabudowy* to contradict the *Studium*. Argues that true *Ład Przestrzenny* (spatial order) is a harmony of systems, not just aesthetics.

## In the Sources
- Interviewed in Kwartalnik RZUT, providing a deep critique of power imbalances in municipal planning, the marginalization of urbanists, and the legal paradoxes of the WZ decision in [[rzut-nielad-w-prawie]].
