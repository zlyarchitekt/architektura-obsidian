## Overview

Dr. Monika Wróbel is a Polish architect and urbanist associated with the Fundacja Skwer Sportów Miejskich (Urban Sports Meadow Foundation), a Warsaw-based foundation focused on public recreational spaces. She appears in RZUT-33 as an external commentator on Warsaw's new Studium, offering a practitioner's perspective independent of [[BAiPP]]. Her primary contribution is grounding the Studium's green-first approach in observable practice: the Foundation's projects confirm that residents' demand for urban greenery has grown year-on-year.

**PL:** Dr Monika Wróbel jest architektką i urbanistką, związaną z Fundacją Skwer Sportów Miejskich — fundacją zajmującą się publicznymi przestrzeniami rekreacyjnymi w Warszawie. W RZUT-33 komentuje nowe Studium jako zewnętrzny praktyk.

## Ideas & Influences

Wróbel values the **ecosystem framing** of Warsaw's new Studium as a governance innovation — the cross-sectoral vision where "individual sectors see each other and work toward a common goal" enables integrated planning rather than siloed departmentalism. She also endorses the **locality agenda** (local centres as hearts of communities, delivering social equity through fair access to services) and the **"wszędzie blisko"** (everywhere close) ideal as the most publicly resonant planning vision.

She predicts that **car restriction** will be the main axis of controversy in public reception of the Studium — an important forecast from an external practitioner who observes urban residents directly.
