## Overview

The Międzynarodowe Centrum Kongresowe (MCK) in Katowice, completed 2015, is JEMS Architekci's most significant civic project and the most awarded single building in the wiki. Won in an open SARP competition in 2008, the building serves as the primary meeting and congress venue for the Silesia region and for international events. Its compact, dark, horizontally resolved mass — clad in a characteristic dark facade — makes a strong contextual gesture within the post-industrial Katowice Strefa Kultury urban complex (alongside NOSPR and Muzeum Śląskie).
